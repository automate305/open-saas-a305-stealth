# SOP: Warranty Policy

> Sample warranty policy. Replace terms with the shop's real warranty before production.

## Labor warranty
- **Repairs:** 90-day labor warranty on any repair we perform.
- **New system installs:** 2-year labor warranty (10-year on select premium installs).

## Parts warranty
- Manufacturer parts warranty typically **5–10 years** with product registration.
- We register the equipment for the customer at time of install.

## What's covered
- Failure of a part we installed, under normal use, within the warranty window.
- Return visit at no labor charge for a warrantied repair that recurs.

## What's NOT covered
- Damage from lack of maintenance, power surges, flooding, or third-party tampering.
- Filters and consumables.

## AI handling
- If a customer asks "is this covered under warranty?" — pull this doc, confirm the window,
  and if it's ambiguous, book a warranty-check visit rather than promising coverage.
- Never promise a free repair without confirming the install date is on file in GoHighLevel.
