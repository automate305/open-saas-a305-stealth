# Call Transcript — WON (Booked Same-Day) — winning-call-001

> Sample transcript used as a pattern for how a good booking call flows.
> Outcome: **Booked same-day AC repair, $89 diagnostic waived on repair.**

**AI:** Thanks for calling [Shop Name], this is your AI assistant — how can I help today?
**Customer:** My AC is blowing hot air and it's brutal in here.
**AI:** Sorry you're dealing with that in this heat — I can get a tech out today. Is this for
your home or a business?
**Customer:** Home.
**AI:** Got it. What's the address, and the best callback number?
**Customer:** 1420 SW 8th St, and my cell is 305-555-0142.
**AI:** Perfect. Blowing hot usually means a refrigerant or capacitor issue — the tech will
diagnose it on site. The diagnostic is $89 and it's waived if you book the repair the same
visit. I've got a 2pm or a 4pm open today — which works better?
**Customer:** 2pm.
**AI:** Done — you're booked for 2pm today, [date]. I'm sending a text confirmation now with
the tech's ETA. Anything else?
**Customer:** Nope, thank you.

## Pattern notes (why it worked)
- Led with empathy, moved fast to qualify (home/business → address → phone).
- Named the likely issue but did NOT quote a fixed repair price — deferred to on-site.
- Used the $89-waived line to lower resistance.
- Offered exactly **two** concrete slots (choice, not open-ended).
- Confirmed booking + SMS in one motion.
