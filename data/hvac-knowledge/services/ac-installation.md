# Service: AC Installation / Replacement

> Sample service description. Replace brands, tonnage, and pricing with the shop's real data.

## Overview
Full residential AC system replacement — condenser, air handler/coil, and thermostat, sized
to the home with a proper load calculation.

## Timeline
- **Standard single-system swap:** typically completed in **one day**.
- **Add-ons** (new ductwork, electrical, permits): may add 1–2 days.

## What's included
- In-home consult + load sizing (free for installs)
- Removal & haul-away of the old system
- New system install + startup test
- Equipment registration for warranty [Source: sops/warranty-policy.md]

## Pricing approach
- Installs are quoted after the free consult (varies by tonnage/efficiency/home).
- Financing: monthly payment plans available [Source: pricing/ac-repair-2024.md § Financing].
- The AI should **never quote a flat install price** — always book the free consult.

## Good-fit signals for the AI to book a consult
- "My unit is 12+ years old / keeps breaking"
- "My energy bills are really high"
- "I've repaired it 2–3 times this year"
