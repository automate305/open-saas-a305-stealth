# Service: Equipment Specs (Chatbot Photo-Diagnosis Reference)

> Sample equipment reference used by the AI Chatbot when a customer uploads a photo.
> Used only to give a **rough** direction + estimate range — never a firm diagnosis.

## Common visible symptoms → likely direction
| What the photo shows | Likely direction | Rough starting range | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ice on the copper lines / coil | Low refrigerant or airflow issue | $150–$450 | pricing/ac-repair-2024.md § Refrigerant recharge |
| Swollen/bulging capacitor (top domed) | Capacitor replacement | $129–$249 | pricing/ac-repair-2024.md § Capacitor |
| Rusted/corroded contactor | Contactor replacement | $149–$299 | pricing/ac-repair-2024.md § Contactor |
| Unit not spinning / fan stalled | Fan motor or capacitor | $129–$650 | pricing/ac-repair-2024.md |
| Standing water around air handler | Clogged drain / coil issue | diagnostic needed | pricing/ac-repair-2024.md § Diagnostic Fee |
| Old rusty condenser, 12+ yrs | Replacement candidate | free consult | services/ac-installation.md |

## Efficiency reference (for install conversations)
- **SEER2 14.3** = current minimum efficiency baseline (South region).
- Higher SEER2 = higher upfront cost, lower monthly bills — quoted at consult.

## Rules for the AI
- A photo gives a **direction and a range**, not a firm quote. Always follow with:
  "The tech will confirm exactly on site."
- If the photo is unclear or the symptom isn't in this table → book a diagnostic, don't guess.
